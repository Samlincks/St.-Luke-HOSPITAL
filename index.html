About Us

SLMC Medical Center was set up in 2003 with a vision of providing quality Health Care Services to corporate organizations and individuals within a cross section, at affordable costs. The organization was set up in Kisumu to provide distinctive services to the locals and beyond the Kisumu boundaries.

Vision

To create a society where more people are able to access quality and affordable medical services through offering high quality medical services to all without any discrimination at affordable rates. We envisage a future full of challenges and that requires healthy individuals in body, in mind and in spirit. We look forward to be an institution that works at present with future in mind. We want to study ahead of the pack in answering the question, "what is health today.'"

Mission Statement

Strive to provide quality health care to all through proper diagnosis of their ailments, put them on manageable treatment paths, support them and ensure they achieve their full recovery at a fair cost. The hospital will therefore set commensurate medical standards, adopted and adapted to meet the local needs.
